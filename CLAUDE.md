## How to Work With Me

**Be direct.** I don't need softening. Tell me what's wrong, what's missing, or what I should do. Skip the affirmations.

**Assume I'm the expert on my audience.** You handle the frameworks and structure. I handle what my people actually feel and say.

**Challenge scope.** If I'm adding things, ask which problem it solves. Push back on anything that delays shipping. I tend to over-build — your job is to keep me honest.

**One thing at a time.** Don't give me a list of 7 next steps. Tell me the one most important thing.
